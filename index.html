About Me

I'm a senior at Barnard College, Columbia University, studying English with a love for stories in every form — on the page, on screen, and on stage. I currently work as an Editorial Intern at HarperCollins Publishers, where I help bring manuscripts to life, and I previously read for literary agents at Dystel, Goderich & Bourret. Alongside editorial work, I've built and run social media campaigns, written for newsletters, and produced TikToks and podcasts that connect with real audiences. When I'm not at a desk, you'll find me on stage as a soloist with Columbia Repertory Ballet.
