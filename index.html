About Me

Hi, I'm Laine! I'm a recent graduate of Barnard College, where I studied English and Dance. My work lives at the intersection of storytelling, publishing, and digital media, driven by a lifelong love of books and the communities they create.

I've had the opportunity to work with HarperCollins Publishers, Three Rooms Press, and Dystel, Goderich & Bourret, experiences that deepened my passion for editorial publishing while giving me insight into every stage of a book's journey.

Whether I'm reading a manuscript, creating content that helps readers discover their next favorite book, or writing about literature, I'm always drawn to stories that challenge, inspire, and stay with us long after the final page. Thanks for stopping by!
